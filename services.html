Our Services

  • Diabetic Retinopathy Severity Level Determination: Our specialized service focuses on determining the severity level of diabetic retinopathy, providing crucial insights for patient management and treatment planning. The severity levels include:
  • 0 - No diabetic retinopathy
  • 1 - Mild non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy (DR)
  • 2 - Moderate non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy (DR)
  • 3 - Severe non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy (DR)
  • 4 - Proliferative diabetic retinopathy (DR)
